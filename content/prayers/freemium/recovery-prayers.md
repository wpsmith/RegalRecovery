# Recovery-Focused Prayers

**Source:** App Original
**Pack:** Core Prayers
**Tier:** Free

---

## Prayer for Courage
**Scripture:** Joshua 1:9

Lord, I need Your courage today. The path of recovery is hard, and there are moments when I want to turn back. Remind me that You have not given me a spirit of fear, but of power, love, and a sound mind. I choose to walk forward, one step at a time.

---

## Prayer of Gratitude in Recovery
**Scripture:** 1 Thessalonians 5:18

Father, thank You for this day of sobriety. Thank You for the people who walk beside me. Thank You for the strength that is not my own. Even in the hard moments, I choose gratitude. Every sober day is a miracle, and I do not take it for granted.

---

## Prayer for Forgiveness
**Scripture:** Psalm 51:10

God, create in me a clean heart. I have sinned against You and against those I love. The weight of guilt is heavy, but Your grace is heavier. I receive Your forgiveness today, not because I deserve it, but because You are merciful. Help me to forgive myself as You have forgiven me.

---

## Prayer for Strength
**Scripture:** Isaiah 40:29-31

Lord, I am weary. Recovery demands more than I have to give. But You promise to give strength to the weary and increase the power of the weak. I wait on You today. Renew my strength so I can run without growing weary and walk without fainting.

---

## Prayer of Surrender
**Scripture:** Matthew 11:28-30

Jesus, I am tired of carrying this alone. I come to You with my burdens, my shame, my fears, my addiction. Take them. Your yoke is easy and Your burden is light. I stop fighting and start resting in You.
