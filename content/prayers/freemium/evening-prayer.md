# Evening Prayer for Recovery

**Source:** App Original
**Pack:** Core Prayers
**Tier:** Free
**Scripture:** Psalm 4:8

---

Father, as this day comes to a close, I come before You with an honest heart.

Thank You for the strength You gave me today. Where I honored my commitments, I give You the glory. Where I fell short, I ask for Your forgiveness and the courage to try again tomorrow.

I release the shame of the past and the anxiety of the future. Tonight, I rest in Your grace. I am not defined by my worst moments. I am defined by Your love.

Guard my sleep. Quiet the thoughts that race. Fill the empty spaces with Your peace so that I wake ready to walk another day in freedom.

In peace I will lie down and sleep, for You alone, Lord, make me dwell in safety.

Amen.
