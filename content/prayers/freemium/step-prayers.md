# 12-Step Prayers

**Source:** App Original
**Pack:** Step Prayers
**Tier:** Free

---

## Step 1: Admitting Powerlessness
**Scripture:** 2 Corinthians 12:9-10

God, I admit that I am powerless over my addiction. My life has become unmanageable. I cannot fix myself. I need You. Help me accept this truth without shame, knowing that admitting weakness is the first step toward Your strength.

---

## Step 2: Believing in Restoration
**Scripture:** Jeremiah 29:11

Father, I believe You can restore me to sanity. When my thinking is twisted and my desires are disordered, You alone can make me whole. I choose to believe that You have a good plan for my life, even when I cannot see it.

---

## Step 3: Turning Over My Will
**Scripture:** Proverbs 3:5-6

Lord, I turn my will and my life over to Your care. I stop trying to control what I cannot control. I trust You with my recovery, my relationships, my future. Take the steering wheel. I surrender.

---

## Step 4: Courage for Moral Inventory
**Scripture:** Psalm 139:23-24

God, give me the courage to look honestly at myself. Search me and know my anxious thoughts. Show me the resentments, the fears, the harm I have caused. Let me see the truth without running from it.

---

## Step 5: Honesty Before God and Others
**Scripture:** James 5:16

Father, give me the courage to confess my wrongs to You and to another person. Break the power of secrecy over my life. Let honesty be my foundation, even when it is painful.

---

## Step 6: Readiness for Change
**Scripture:** Ezekiel 36:26

Lord, I am ready for You to remove my defects of character. I do not have the power to change myself, but I am willing. Give me a new heart. Take away my heart of stone.

---

## Step 7: Humility Before God
**Scripture:** 1 Peter 5:6

God, I humbly ask You to remove my shortcomings. I come to You without pretense. I am broken, but You are a healer. I am weak, but You are strong. Do the work in me that I cannot do myself.

---

## Step 8: Willingness to Make Amends
**Scripture:** Matthew 5:23-24

Father, make me willing to make amends to those I have hurt. Soften the hardened places in my heart. Replace my pride with humility, my fear with courage, my avoidance with action.

---

## Step 9: Making Direct Amends
**Scripture:** Romans 12:18

Lord, give me wisdom as I make amends. Help me to do so without causing further harm. Where reconciliation is possible, let it begin. Where it is not, help me to release those situations to You.

---

## Step 10: Daily Inventory
**Scripture:** 1 John 1:8-9

God, help me to continue taking personal inventory each day. When I am wrong, let me promptly admit it. Keep me honest. Keep me humble. Do not let me slip back into denial.

---

## Step 11: Conscious Contact with God
**Scripture:** Psalm 46:10

Father, through prayer and meditation, I seek to improve my conscious contact with You. Teach me to be still. Teach me to listen. Show me Your will for my life and give me the power to carry it out.

---

## Step 12: Carrying the Message
**Scripture:** Galatians 6:1-2

Lord, having experienced Your transforming grace, help me carry this message to others who still suffer. Let my story be a testimony of Your power. Use my weakness for Your glory.
