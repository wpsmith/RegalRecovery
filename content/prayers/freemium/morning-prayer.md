# Morning Prayer for Recovery

**Source:** App Original
**Pack:** Core Prayers
**Tier:** Free
**Scripture:** Lamentations 3:22-23

---

Lord, as I begin this new day, I thank You that Your mercies are new every morning. Great is Your faithfulness.

I surrender this day to You. I cannot face it alone, and I choose not to try. Guard my eyes, my heart, and my mind. Where temptation meets me, give me the strength to turn away and the humility to call for help.

Let me walk in honesty today -- with You, with myself, and with those who walk beside me. Remind me that sobriety is a gift, not an achievement. Help me receive it with gratitude.

If I stumble, catch me. If I fall, lift me up. You have not brought me this far to leave me now.

In Jesus' name, Amen.
