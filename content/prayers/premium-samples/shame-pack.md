# Shame & Identity Prayer Pack (Samples)

**Pack ID:** pack_shame
**Tier:** Premium

---

## Prayer for Release from Shame
**Scripture:** Romans 8:1

Father, the voice of shame is loud today. It tells me I am worthless, that I will never change, that I am defined by my worst moments. But Your Word says there is no condemnation for those who are in Christ Jesus. I reject the lie of shame and receive Your truth. I am forgiven. I am free.

---

## Prayer for Identity in Christ
**Scripture:** 2 Corinthians 5:17

God, remind me who I am in You. I am a new creation. The old has gone, the new has come. My identity is not "addict." My identity is "beloved child of God." Help me live from that truth today, not from the broken labels the world puts on me.

---

## Prayer Against Condemnation
**Scripture:** Isaiah 43:25

Lord, You are the one who blots out my transgressions for Your own sake. You choose not to remember my sins. If You have forgotten them, help me stop replaying them. Free me from the prison of self-condemnation. Let me walk in the freedom You have already purchased for me.
