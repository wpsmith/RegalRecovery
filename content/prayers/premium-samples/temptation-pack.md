# Temptation & Urges Prayer Pack (Samples)

**Pack ID:** pack_temptation
**Tier:** Premium

---

## Prayer for Strength Against Temptation
**Scripture:** 1 Corinthians 10:13

Heavenly Father, I come to You in my weakness. The pull of temptation is strong, but Your strength is stronger. You have promised that no temptation will seize me except what is common to humanity, and that You will provide a way out. Show me that way right now. I choose You over the lie that my addiction offers. Help me flee and not linger.

---

## Prayer When Urges Are Strong
**Scripture:** James 4:7-8

God, the urge is here and it feels overwhelming. I resist it in Your name. I submit myself to You. I draw near to You, and I trust that You are drawing near to me. Replace this craving with Your presence. Let this moment pass without me acting on it. I am not my urges. I am Your child.

---

## Prayer for Purity of Thought
**Scripture:** Philippians 4:8

Lord, cleanse my mind. Whatever is true, noble, right, pure, lovely, admirable -- fix my thoughts on these things. When intrusive thoughts invade, replace them with Your truth. I take every thought captive and make it obedient to Christ.
