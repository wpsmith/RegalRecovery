# Marriage Restoration Prayer Pack (Samples)

**Pack ID:** pack_marriage
**Tier:** Premium

---

## Prayer for My Spouse's Healing
**Scripture:** Psalm 147:3

God, my addiction has wounded the person I love most. I cannot undo the damage, but You can heal. Bind up my spouse's broken heart. Comfort them in ways I cannot. Give them the support and community they need. Let my recovery be the beginning of their healing, not another source of pain.

---

## Prayer for Trust Restoration
**Scripture:** Proverbs 3:3-4

Father, I have broken trust. It took moments to destroy and will take a lifetime to rebuild. Give me patience. Give me consistency. Help me show through my actions, not just my words, that I am changing. And give my spouse the grace to see the changes, in Your time, not mine.

---

## Prayer for Honest Communication
**Scripture:** Ephesians 4:25

Lord, teach me to speak the truth in love. For too long, I hid behind lies and half-truths. Break the habit of deception in me. Give me the courage to be fully honest, even when it is uncomfortable. Help us build our marriage on a foundation of truth and transparency.
